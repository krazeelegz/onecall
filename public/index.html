DECOMMISSIONING


Providing a fully integrated approach to well decommissioning from feasibility studies and front end engineering through to detailed planning and management of the operation, we have the depth and breadth of knowledge to tackle a wide range of scenarios.

From the simple, requiring only light intervention vessels, to the more complex, we will safely execute your well decommissioning project in full compliance with all regulatory requirements.

We support you by:

  • Fully reviewing and evaluating the current status of wells identified for decommissioning
  • Determining the most cost-effective method to assure well integrity prior to initial suspension
  • Programming and executing full abandonment and recovery of sub-sea hardware hardware associated with the wells
  • Safely re-entering a well
  • Providing safe and effective containment of well pressures and hydrocarbon inventor
  • Ensuring sub-sea equipment is effectively terminated and safely recovered to surface for onshore disposal or recommending methods for reducing waste disposal requirement
  • Providing the resources to prepare the necessary regulatory HSE submissions and DECC notifications
  • Proactively interfacing with pipelines, platforms, asset integrity and other facilities providers to ensure a joined-up approach to your field decommissioning requirements.
